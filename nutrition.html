Nutrition of Baby

Proper nutrition plays a vital role in your baby’s physical and mental development. Below are general guidelines for different age groups:

  • 0 - 6 months:
    • Exclusive breastfeeding is highly recommended.
    • Breast milk provides all the nutrients your baby needs.
  • 6 - 12 months:
    • Continue breastfeeding while introducing soft solid foods.
    • Start with pureed fruits, vegetables, and iron-fortified cereals.
    • Gradually add mashed foods and small soft finger foods.
  • 12 - 24 months:
    • Introduce a variety of foods with different textures and flavors.
    • Include fruits, vegetables, grains, dairy, and protein-rich foods.
    • Avoid sugar-sweetened beverages and junk food.

Always consult your pediatrician before introducing new foods or supplements to your baby’s diet.
